Fictional Company Remote Work Policy (demo document)

Note: "Aster Robotics" is a fictional company invented for this demo. Any
resemblance to a real company is coincidental. This document exists so you
can test DocuChat's grounded Q&A on a realistic-looking policy document.

1. Eligibility
   Full-time employees who have completed a 90-day probation period are
   eligible to work remotely up to 3 days per week, subject to manager
   approval.

2. Core hours
   Remote employees must be reachable between 11:00 and 16:00 in their
   local time zone, regardless of which days they work remotely.

3. Equipment
   Aster Robotics provides a laptop and a one-time home-office stipend of
   $400. Employees are responsible for their own internet connection.

4. Expense reimbursement
   Reasonable co-working space costs (up to $150/month) are reimbursable
   with a submitted receipt. Personal equipment upgrades are not covered.

5. Security
   Remote employees must use the company VPN when accessing internal
   systems and must not work from public, unsecured Wi-Fi networks without
   the VPN enabled.

6. Review
   This policy is reviewed annually by HR every January.

Try asking: "How many days per week can employees work remotely at Aster
Robotics?" or "What is the home-office stipend?"
